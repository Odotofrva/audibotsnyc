The AudiBots story

Share the origin of the club, the hardships that shaped it, and the renewed vision behind building something stronger in New York City through cars, innovation, and positive community.

2017 Beginning

Where AudiBots started

AudiBots began in 2017 when I had my Audi A5. I drove it everywhere and built a real connection with the car through experience, curiosity, and the desire to understand it beyond just driving.

Hands-On Learning

Passion turned into maintenance and diagnostics

That passion pushed me to learn how to handle much of the maintenance myself. Over time, I also created OBD-based systems to diagnose the cars, blending automotive culture with technical problem-solving and self-taught innovation.

Hurricane Helene

When the community took a major hit

That momentum was deeply disrupted after Hurricane Helene, when major flooding damaged vehicles and impacted many people connected to the community. What had been built started to fall apart under circumstances far bigger than cars alone.

New York City Chapter

Rebuilding with a bigger purpose

Now in NYC, the goal is to push AudiBots in a greater direction by merging community, innovation, technology, and car culture into something uplifting. It is about building a stronger network, inspiring positive lifestyle choices, and showing that passion can evolve into real impact.

Why AudiBots exists

AudiBots exists to bring people together through a shared love for Audi while opening the door to something deeper than just showing cars. It is about learning, building, creating, and pushing the culture forward through knowledge, resilience, and positive community energy.

The direction ahead

The next chapter for AudiBots is focused on deeper member spotlights, stronger event experiences, educational tech around diagnostics and builds, and a digital platform that reflects the future of a smarter, more connected car community in New York City.

Join AudiBots

Whether you are an owner, supporter, builder, or creative, AudiBots is a place to connect through cars, technology, and forward movement. Join the next chapter and help shape a community built on passion, resilience, and positive growth.
